This isn't about making reckless career moves. It's about smart, calculated transitions that leverage your existing expertise while building toward entrepreneurial independence.

I work with clients to assess their unique situations, identify transferable skills, evaluate market opportunities, and create actionable transition timelines. Every strategy is customized to your specific circumstances, risk tolerance, and long-term objectives.

## My Approach

I believe in adult-to-adult communication and practical, reality-grounded advice. My consulting approach is built on several core principles:

**Authenticity Over Perfection**: I share both successes and failures honestly. My failed entrepreneurial ventures taught me as much as my corporate achievements, and those lessons inform my guidance.

**Strategy Over Motivation**: While encouragement has its place, I focus on developing concrete strategies and actionable plans rather than motivational speeches.

**Evidence-Based Decisions**: Drawing from my engineering background, I emphasize data-driven decision-making and systematic approaches to complex challenges.

**Long-Term Thinking**: Professional transitions aren't quick fixes. I help clients develop sustainable strategies that build value over time while managing immediate practical concerns.

**Values-Driven Success**: True professional freedom comes from aligning your work with your core values. I help clients identify and pursue opportunities that provide both financial success and personal fulfillment.
