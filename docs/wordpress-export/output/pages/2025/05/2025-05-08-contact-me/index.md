- Looking for practical advice on building skills and networks for transition

- Seeking accountability and support during their planning phase

## Professional Boundaries

While I'm committed to helping fellow professionals navigate these challenging transitions, please note that I provide perspective and guidance based on experience, not formal coaching or financial advisory  
services. For legal, tax, or investment decisions, I recommend consulting with qualified professionals.

Your contact information and any details you share remain strictly confidential. I never share personal communications or use specific situations as content examples without explicit permission. Your trust  
in reaching out is something I take seriously. I look forward to connecting with you and supporting your journey toward professional and entrepreneurial freedom.
